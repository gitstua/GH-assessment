Welcome to GH Well-Architected (unofficial)

GH Well-Architected is a set of assessments to prompt discussions around configuring secure, high-performing, resilient, and efficient GitHub.

FAQ

Q: How do I get started?

A: Click on one of the assessments above to get started.

Q: How do I export my results?

A: Click on the "Export to CSV" button at the end of the assessment.

Q: How do I reset my results?

A: Click on the "Reset" button at the end of the assessment.

Q: How do I change my answers?

A: Press previous. At this time you need to reset and restart once you have completed the survey.

Q: Does this replace all the smart people in my team and those who help us from GitHub?

A: No. This is a tool to help you have a conversation with your team and GitHub. It is not a replacement for the smart people you work with. You should use this to start proactive conversations with these people around what meets your personal goals with GitHub.

Q: What if I think something is wrong with the content or have suggestions?

A: Please raise an issue on the repo - be sure to search in case this issue has already been raised.
